Our Story

Born From a Personal Mission

Founded by Maria Rivera, daughter of Cuban immigrants who arrived in Miami with little more than hope and determination, Rivera Law PLLC was built on a deeply personal foundation.

Maria saw firsthand the fear and uncertainty her family faced navigating the immigration system alone. She became an attorney not just to practice law, but to be the guide her family never had. Today, her firm serves thousands of families across South Florida with that same unwavering care.
