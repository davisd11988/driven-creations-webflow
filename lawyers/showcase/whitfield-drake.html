02 — Practice Areas

Comprehensive IP
Legal Coverage

From the patent application through final grant — and every enforcement action in between — our practice is built around the full lifecycle of intellectual property protection.

Patent Prosecution

Strategic drafting and prosecution of utility, design, and provisional patent applications before the USPTO and international IP offices under the PCT framework. We work with engineers and inventors to craft claims that maximize protection and withstand PTAB scrutiny.

USPTO · PCT · PTAB

Trademark Registration

End-to-end trademark clearance, application filing, and maintenance for brand names, logos, and product identifiers across all international classes. We manage opposition proceedings, cancellation actions, and international registrations under the Madrid Protocol.

USPTO · TTAB · Madrid Protocol

Trade Secret Litigation

Aggressive prosecution and defense of trade secret misappropriation claims under the Defend Trade Secrets Act (DTSA) and California Uniform Trade Secrets Act (CUTSA). From TRO motions to full jury trials, we protect your most sensitive proprietary information.

DTSA · CUTSA · N.D. Cal.

IP Portfolio Strategy

Holistic portfolio audits, freedom-to-operate opinions, IP due diligence for M&A transactions, and licensing strategy for technology companies. We align your IP assets with long-term business objectives and fundraising milestones to maximize enterprise valuation.

FTO · M&A DD · Licensing
03 — About the Firm

Built for the
Innovation Economy

Founded in 2009 by Marcus Whitfield and Claire Drake — both formerly of Fenwick & West's IP group — Whitfield & Drake was established on a single conviction: that the best intellectual property counsel doesn't just file papers. It thinks like an engineer, reads the market like a VC, and fights like a litigator when the moment demands it.

Our attorneys hold graduate degrees in computer science, electrical engineering, and biotechnology alongside their JDs. That technical depth means we understand not just the legal landscape, but the inventions themselves — enabling us to draft stronger claims, spot infringement faster, and advise founders with the clarity their investors expect.

From a converted warehouse in SoMa, we serve over 200 active clients ranging from stealth-mode startups to publicly traded technology companies, managing portfolios spanning software patents, industrial design, and biotech trade secrets.

Technical Depth

Every attorney holds an advanced STEM degree — we speak your inventors' language fluently.

Startup-Aligned Pricing

Flexible engagement models designed around funding stages — from pre-seed capped retainers to full portfolio management agreements.

Aggressive Enforcement

When your IP is threatened, we move fast — securing TROs, issuing cease and desist letters, and escalating to federal court when necessary.
